Common Payment Issues

Card declines can happen for several reasons:

  • Insufficient funds in your account
  • Card has expired or is not activated
  • Incorrect card details entered
  • Your bank's fraud protection triggered

Solution: Contact your bank or try a different payment method.

If you're using an international card:

  • Ensure international transactions are enabled
  • Check with your bank about overseas spending limits
  • Verify your billing address matches your bank records

Currency: All payments are processed in SGD (Singapore Dollars).

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser or device
  • Disable browser extensions temporarily
  • Check your internet connection is stable

Still having issues? Contact our support team for assistance.
